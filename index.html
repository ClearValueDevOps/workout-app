Nutrition for Fat Loss

The Truth About Stomach Fat

You cannot spot-reduce fat from your stomach. Fat loss happens throughout your entire body when you maintain a caloric deficit. Core exercises build muscle underneath, but nutrition is the key to revealing those muscles.

Key Principles

Caloric Deficit

  • • Eat 300-500 calories below maintenance
  • • Track with apps like MyFitnessPal
  • • Aim for 1-2 lbs loss per week

Protein Priority

  • • 0.7-1g per lb bodyweight
  • • Preserves muscle while losing fat
  • • Keeps you full longer

Hydration

  • • Drink 8-10 glasses daily
  • • Reduces water retention
  • • Supports metabolism

Whole Foods

  • • Lean proteins
  • • Vegetables & fruits
  • • Whole grains
  • • Healthy fats

Weekly Meal Plan (Eating Out Friendly)

These meals can be ordered from restaurants, picked up from grocery stores, or meal prep services. Focus on portion control and protein.

Monday - High Protein Day

Breakfast: Starbucks Egg White & Roasted Red Pepper Egg Bites (2 orders) + Black coffee

Lunch: Chipotle Bowl: Chicken, brown rice (half scoop), fajita veggies, lettuce, pico, no cheese/sour cream

Dinner: Grilled salmon or chicken breast from any restaurant + steamed vegetables + side salad

Snacks: Premier Protein shake (pre-made), apple, almonds (small handful)

Tuesday - Balanced Day

Breakfast: Greek yogurt (Fage or Chobani) + berries + granola (small amount)

Lunch: Subway: 6" Turkey Breast on wheat, loaded with veggies, mustard (no mayo)

Dinner: Rotisserie chicken (grocery store) + pre-made Caesar salad (light dressing) + sweet potato

Snacks: String cheese, baby carrots with hummus (store-bought)

Wednesday - Asian Inspired

Breakfast: Dunkin' Egg & Cheese Wake-Up Wrap (2) + Coffee

Lunch: Panda Express: Grilled Teriyaki Chicken + Super Greens + Mixed Veggies (skip rice or half portion)

Dinner: Sushi: Sashimi (12 pieces) or poke bowl with brown rice (small scoop), lots of veggies

Snacks: Edamame (grocery store), protein bar (Quest or RX Bar)

Thursday - Mediterranean

Breakfast: Panera Avocado Egg White & Spinach breakfast sandwich

Lunch: Mediterranean restaurant: Chicken or beef kebab + Greek salad + small hummus (skip pita or have half)

Dinner: Grilled chicken gyro (open-face, one pita) + side of veggies + tzatziki

Snacks: Greek yogurt, mixed nuts (1/4 cup), cucumber slices

Friday - American Comfort

Breakfast: McDonald's Egg McMuffin (no cheese if cutting more calories) + orange juice (small)

Lunch: Any deli: Turkey & avocado sandwich on wheat, hold mayo, add mustard + side salad

Dinner: Steak house: 6oz sirloin or filet + broccoli + house salad (dressing on side)

Snacks: Beef jerky, protein shake, apple

Saturday - Meal Prep Pick-Up

Breakfast: Smoothie bowl from local juice bar (protein powder added, light on granola)

Lunch: Whole Foods hot bar: Grilled chicken, roasted vegetables, quinoa (small portion)

Dinner: Meal prep service container (examples: Factor, Trifecta, Freshly) - choose high protein option

Snacks: Pre-cut fruit from grocery store, handful of almonds

Sunday - Flex Day

Breakfast: IHOP: Simple & Fit 2-Egg Breakfast with turkey sausage + fruit

Lunch: Salad bar (grocery store): Load up on greens, chicken, hard-boiled eggs, veggies. Light dressing.

Dinner: Your choice! Pick your favorite restaurant but follow the smart ordering rules below

Snacks: Protein yogurt, trail mix (small portion)

Smart Ordering Rules

  • ✓ Always ask for: Grilled (not fried), dressing on the side, extra vegetables
  • ✓ Protein first: Order chicken, fish, lean beef, turkey, eggs
  • ✓ Half portions: Ask for half rice, half pasta, or share with someone
  • ✓ Swap smartly: Replace fries with salad or steamed veggies
  • ✓ Skip or limit: Bread basket, sugary drinks, creamy sauces, fried foods
  • ✓ Restaurant apps: Check nutrition info before ordering

Best Fast Food Options

Chipotle: Burrito bowl, chicken/steak, brown rice (half), black beans, fajita veggies, salsa, lettuce

Chick-fil-A: Grilled chicken sandwich or grilled nuggets + side salad + fruit cup

Subway: 6" turkey or chicken on wheat, all veggies, mustard/vinegar

Panera: Any "You Pick 2" with soup + half salad with chicken

Sweetgreen/Salad chains: Warm protein bowl or custom salad, extra protein

Grocery Store Quick Picks

Proteins: Rotisserie chicken, pre-cooked grilled chicken strips, hard-boiled eggs (pre-made), canned tuna/salmon, deli turkey breast

Ready-to-Eat: Pre-made salads (check protein), veggie trays with hummus, Greek yogurt cups, protein bars

Quick Sides: Microwavable brown rice pouches, steamable veggie bags, pre-cut fruit, bagged salads

Drinks: Water (always), unsweetened tea, black coffee, zero-calorie drinks, protein shakes (pre-made)

What to Limit/Avoid

  • • Sugary drinks (soda, sweetened coffee drinks, juice)
  • • Deep-fried foods (fried chicken, french fries, onion rings)
  • • Excessive alcohol (beer, cocktails - high calories)
  • • Creamy sauces & dressings (ranch, mayo, alfredo)
  • • Large portions of refined carbs (white bread, pasta, white rice)
  • • Fast food breakfast sandwiches with sausage/bacon
  • • Pizza (unless thin crust with veggies, small portion)
  • • Desserts & pastries (save for occasional treat)

Meal Timing Tips

Pre-Workout (30-60 min before): Light meal with carbs + protein. Example: Banana + protein shake, or small turkey sandwich

Post-Workout (within 1-2 hours): Protein-rich meal. Example: Chipotle bowl, grilled chicken with rice, protein shake + meal

Between Meals: High-protein snacks keep you full. Example: Greek yogurt, protein bar, jerky, hard-boiled eggs

Evening: Lighter on carbs, focus on protein + veggies. Example: Grilled fish + salad, chicken + roasted vegetables

Portion Control When Eating Out

  • Protein: Palm-sized portion (about 4-6 oz)
  • Carbs: Fist-sized portion (rice, pasta, potatoes)
  • Vegetables: Unlimited! Fill half your plate
  • Fats: Thumb-sized (dressings, oils, butter) - they're calorie-dense
  • Restaurant trick: Ask for a to-go box with your meal, box half immediately
